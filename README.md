Name : **"UNLUCKY COMMUTOR"**

A story of a person who is desperately unlucky and this kinda fortune happens only to him
and here we granted some limited Superpowers which we gonna use to save him..
And Make Him fortunate and realize him that "Bad times or Good Times always stay in limit" .

**INSTRUCTIONS** :

User starts the Game by Clicking on _"Start Game"_ button

Then he has the choice to select different Places where 

wants to play, three of them are there :

1. **Metro City**

2. **Airport**

3. **Train**

And Then We after Choosing the Place we have further options of complexity levels

1. **Easy**

2. **Medium**

3. **Difficult**


On Chosing the level modes, Game started ....

 **On Long Press of Backspace key** or **Hold the Right Click** Button on Mouse  to Jump ..
